READING AND WRITING

These two words always go hand in hand. Everywhere you go,these two; reading and writing are used in our daily lives.
If someone wants to relax after a day's work or after taking a test, these two
are always appliable. Reading and writing are hobbies to many, talents to some and not forgetting that to some people, they are responsibilities.
A relaxing environment is not complete when you are lacking a book and a pen accompanied with a cup of coffee or black tea.
For the introverts, writing is a form of telling a story without making eye contact which is honestly tiring. Reading is a form avoiding conversations and for broadening the mind.
A special standing ovation to all the authors in the world who have inspired a lost soul back on track to stand up and be strong.
Writers never die.
